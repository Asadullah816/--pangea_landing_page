Who we are?

Pangea was founded in 1999 from humble beginnings as a gladiola farm, providing Ontario Canada with seasonal cut flowers. When harvests produced excess, Time-sensitive cut-flowers would be shipped overnight to the “Clock” (Royal Flora Holland) in Aalsmeer, Netherlands where they would be sold in a matter of hours to willing buyers from around the world.

It was here that Pangea first began to understand the power and beauty of global trade. In the end, we are all connected. Oceans and time zones may divide our day to day lives, but we all share far more with one and other than we often realize.

Fast forward to present day, Pangea now has offices, warehouse and agriculture enterprises in Canada, Germany and the United Arab Emirates, serving the North American, European and Middle Eastern markets. Our focus is largely around outdoor living, health and activity.
